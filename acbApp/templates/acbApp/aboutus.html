About Us

ABSOLUTE CONSTRUCITON SERVICES is a partnership firm. It was established in year 2021, by Mr. Ehtasham Reza and Ms. Aaliya Naz and has become well established and experienced company in plumbing works. It is well known for providing high quality services and timely project delivery.

Our Vision is to provide clients with highly professional, fast, and reliable solutions that best suit and serve their needs. Our motto is Client’s satisfaction with on time delivery. The company differentiates itself as being a unique entity by providing an excellent quality service.

The Company had enjoyed steady growth based upon quality and consumer satisfaction. At its present level, the company is well placed to ensure that all the benefits associated with larger organization are available, whilst remaining at a size which ensures that client receives personal attention from the Partners on time. Currently we are working as a subcontractor, to execute entire plumbing works onsite for VDB Construction and Prospects Infra Pvt. Ltd.
